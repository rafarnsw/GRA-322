Symbol Motion

This project began by receiving a random letter and number combination. This combo was used to design a logo that was later assigned to a ficticious company where we chose the name, brand attributes, and collatoral that would accompany the brand. Once this was complete, the digital component of the project was to animate the logo in a way that told the story of the brand.
